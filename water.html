Water

AI infrastructure generates significant heat. Many data centers rely on water-dependent cooling methods, especially evaporative systems.

Water is also used indirectly through electricity generation processes that depend on thermal regulation.

Facilities in drought-prone or water-stressed regions can place additional pressure on local supplies.

Water Use Considerations in AI Infrastructure

Variable Influence on Water Demand Sustainability Concern
Cooling Method Determines direct water use Evaporative systems increase consumption
Climate Affects cooling load Hotter climates require more cooling
Regional Water Availability Determines resilience Water-stressed areas face greater risk
Seasonal Demand Patterns Impacts supply stability Peak periods can strain infrastructure
