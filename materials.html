Materials

AI systems rely on advanced semiconductor chips made with rare earth elements, ultrapure water, specialized chemicals, and energy-intensive fabrication.

Chip fabrication facilities generate byproducts that require careful handling and long-term regulation.

AI hardware production depends on mining materials like lithium and cobalt. Rapid hardware replacement cycles also increase electronic waste.

Material and Lifecycle Impact Factors

Component Environmental Issue Long-Term Effect
Rare Earth Mining Habitat disruption and pollution Resource depletion
Semiconductor Fabrication Chemical waste and high energy use Industrial emissions
Hardware Turnover Short device lifecycle Increased e-waste
Recycling Systems Recovery efficiency Waste reduction potential
